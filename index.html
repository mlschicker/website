About Me

I'm a mathematics graduate student specializing in algorithms, discrete mathematics, and optimization. Currently pursuing a Master's degree at Technische Universität Berlin, I focus on mathematical modeling, algorithm design, and computational problem-solving.

My research interests lie at the intersection of theoretical mathematics and practical applications, particularly in optimization, scheduling algorithms, and combinatorial problems. I contribute to open-source projects and collaborate with research institutions to advance computational mathematics.
